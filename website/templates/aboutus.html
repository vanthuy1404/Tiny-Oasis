About us

We always try our best to bring customers the best products

The story of Tiny Oasis shop begins with the owner's passion for plants. He started growing plants at a very young age and always wanted to share this passion with others. With the desire to bring freshness and vibrancy to people's living and working spaces, he decided to open Tiny Oasis in 2020.
At Tiny Oasis, we always update our inventory with the latest and most diverse range of plants to serve our customers' needs. We are committed to providing customers with the highest quality products at reasonable prices. Additionally, we have a team of enthusiastic and experienced staff to assist customers in choosing the right products.

About Store

We specialize in providing a variety of plants and decorative cacti for homes, offices, cafes, restaurants, hotels, and other spaces. We take pride in being one of the most beautiful plant and cactus shops
